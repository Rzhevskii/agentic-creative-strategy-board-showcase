# Demo Fixtures

This repository includes sanitized demo fixtures for stable local review.

## Files
- `fixtures/briefs/demo_brief.json`
- `fixtures/sources/demo_sources.json`
- `fixtures/payloads/demo_board_payload.json`

## Purpose
The fixtures are used to demonstrate the board-first experience without relying on unstable live discovery, external websites, or model availability.

## Safety Notes
- the fixtures are sanitized;
- they do not include private user data;
- they do not require private credentials to inspect.
